Reel Talk

Movie talk, for movie fans


Welcome to Reel Talk! This page is dedicated to those who are Die Hard fans of movies or people who just enjoy catching a flick from time to time.


Visit the About page for backstory behind this website and behind the author of this website.

Take a look at the Directors page to be introduced to and learn about some of my favorite film directors, the movies they have made, and some directors that are similar to them in their own right.

Having a movie night but you or your friends can't make a decision on a film to watch? Why not try the Movie Generator? A randomized algorithm will pick out a movie from our online movie database!

Want to see my opinions on movies I have watched? Look no further! Take a gander at the Reviews page to see if my opinion can convince you to see (or not see) the movies I watch!

The movie making industry always has something going on, to keep up to date with the hustle and bustle, visit the News page to see what developments are happening around the world!

Have any questions? Want to make any reccomendations for me? Want to subscribe to our newsletter for frequent updates in and around the film industry? Visit the page Contact Us to get in touch!
